Notable natives of Louisiana

Over its 214-year history as a state, Louisiana has produced notable figures across politics, business, sports, entertainment, science, military service, and the arts. Some are world-famous; others are remembered mainly by Louisiana residents. This page covers categories to give you a sense of the state's cultural and historical contributions.

Politicians

Louisiana has been represented in the US Senate (2 seats) and US House (proportional to population) throughout its history. The state has also produced governors, mayors, US Supreme Court justices, ambassadors, and cabinet members. State politics typically reflects the broader political lean: Republican-leaning.

Entertainment industry

Louisiana has contributed actors, musicians, comedians, directors, and producers to Hollywood, Broadway, and the music industry. The state is itself a major film-production destination. Jazz has deep roots here. Blues music originated or thrived in the state.

Athletes

Louisiana has produced Hall of Fame athletes across major US sports (baseball, football, basketball, hockey, soccer, golf, tennis, boxing, NASCAR, etc.) as well as Olympic medalists. Pro franchises in the state include teams across NFL, NBA.

Business and tech

Louisiana has produced founders, CEOs, inventors, and investors across many industries. Oil and energy industry leaders. Hospitality industry figures. For tech specifically, California, Washington, Massachusetts, and New York dominate, but every state has contributed to American business history.

Science and academia

Louisiana hosts research universities, science museums, and (in some cases) federal research facilities. Nobel laureates and Pulitzer winners have come from the state — see the Wikipedia "List of Nobel laureates by country" for the breakdown by US state.

Military and civic service

Louisiana residents have served in every American war from the Revolution to today. Veterans, civil rights leaders, and community organizers from Louisiana have shaped both the state and the nation.

Where to find a comprehensive list

For the most thorough, up-to-date list of notable people from Louisiana, see Wikipedia's "List of people from Louisiana" or the state historical society's records. We deliberately don't try to list every notable person here — there are simply too many, and the list changes as new figures emerge.

Common questions

How many presidents were born in Louisiana?

Louisiana has not been the birthplace of any US president, though it may be the home state or burial place of one.

Who are famous people from Louisiana?

Louisiana has produced notable figures across many fields including politics, sports, entertainment, business, science, and the arts. See the Wikipedia page "List of people from Louisiana" for the most comprehensive list.

Are there any notable musicians from Louisiana?

Yes, Louisiana has produced notable musicians across genres. The state has a strong blues tradition. Jazz history is also significant.
