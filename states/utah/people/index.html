Notable natives of Utah

Over its 130-year history as a state, Utah has produced notable figures across politics, business, sports, entertainment, science, military service, and the arts. Some are world-famous; others are remembered mainly by Utah residents. This page covers categories to give you a sense of the state's cultural and historical contributions.

Politicians

Utah has been represented in the US Senate (2 seats) and US House (proportional to population) throughout its history. The state has also produced governors, mayors, US Supreme Court justices, ambassadors, and cabinet members. State politics typically reflects the broader political lean: Republican-leaning.

Entertainment industry

Utah has contributed actors, musicians, comedians, directors, and producers to Hollywood, Broadway, and the music industry.

Athletes

Utah has produced Hall of Fame athletes across major US sports (baseball, football, basketball, hockey, soccer, golf, tennis, boxing, NASCAR, etc.) as well as Olympic medalists. Pro franchises in the state include teams across NBA.

Business and tech

Utah has produced founders, CEOs, inventors, and investors across many industries. Hospitality industry figures. For tech specifically, California, Washington, Massachusetts, and New York dominate, but every state has contributed to American business history.

Science and academia

Utah hosts research universities, science museums, and (in some cases) federal research facilities. Nobel laureates and Pulitzer winners have come from the state — see the Wikipedia "List of Nobel laureates by country" for the breakdown by US state.

Military and civic service

Utah residents have served in every American war from the Revolution to today. Veterans, civil rights leaders, and community organizers from Utah have shaped both the state and the nation.

Where to find a comprehensive list

For the most thorough, up-to-date list of notable people from Utah, see Wikipedia's "List of people from Utah" or the state historical society's records. We deliberately don't try to list every notable person here — there are simply too many, and the list changes as new figures emerge.
