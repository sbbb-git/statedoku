New York on the US map

New York is located in the Northeast region of the United States — specifically the mid atlantic subregion. With an area ranking of #27 of 50, it is a moderately-sized state. The map above highlights New York in gold and color-codes the other 49 states by region.

Key cities

The capital of New York is Albany. The largest city by population is New York City. As is the case with most US states, the capital is not the largest city — this is by design, dating back to the late 18th and 19th centuries when state founders deliberately chose smaller, more centrally located towns to host state government and avoid concentrating political and commercial power in the same place.

Borders and neighbors

New York shares land borders with 5 other US states: Vermont, Massachusetts, Connecticut, New Jersey, Pennsylvania.

New York also shares an international border with Canada — part of the longest international border in the world.
