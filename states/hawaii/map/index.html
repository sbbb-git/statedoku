Hawaii on the US map

Hawaii is located in the West region of the United States — specifically the non contiguous subregion. With an area ranking of #43 of 50, it is one of the smallest US states. The map above highlights Hawaii in gold and color-codes the other 49 states by region.

Key cities

The capital of Hawaii is Honolulu. The largest city by population is Honolulu — same as the capital, which is the case in about 17 of 50 US states.

Borders and neighbors

Hawaii shares no land border with any other US state. It is one of two non-contiguous US states.

Geographic features at a glance

  • Total area rank: #43 of 50
  • Population: 1 to 5 million
  • Coastline: pacific

Non-contiguous status

Hawaii is one of only two non-contiguous US states — separated from the lower 48 by water. It shares no land border with any other US state.

Common questions

Where is Hawaii located?
