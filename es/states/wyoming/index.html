Sobre Wyoming

Wyoming es uno de los 50 estados de Estados Unidos, ubicado en la región Oeste del país. Su capital es Cheyenne. Wyoming fue admitido a la Unión en 1890, lo que lo convierte en el 44º estado.

4 datos curiosos sobre Wyoming

  • Wyoming was the first US state/territory to give women the right to vote (1869) — 50 years before the 19th Amendment.
  • It's the least populous US state (~580k people, fewer than Washington DC).
  • Yellowstone, the world's first national park, is mostly in Wyoming. The Grand Prismatic Spring there is bigger than a football field.
  • The state has more pronghorn antelope than people.
