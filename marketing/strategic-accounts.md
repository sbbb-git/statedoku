# Strategic Twitter accounts to follow — Statedoku

Curated list of **niche, engaged accounts** worth following before launch. Avoid mega-accounts (they don't follow back, can't engage). The targets here have **1k–50k followers**, post daily-ish, and have active replies/likes.

**How to use**: Follow these 30 accounts on May 18. Like 1–2 of their recent tweets. A few will reciprocate; that builds the seed audience.

## 🎯 Engagement playbook (no automation)

1. **Mon May 18 (15 min)**: Follow these 30. Like 1–2 recent tweets each.
2. **Daily during launch week (5 min/day)**: Reply genuinely to 3 of their posts. Not "great post 🔥" — actual thoughts.
3. **May 25 (launch day)**: Quote-tweet **one** of their tweets with "Hey [name], you might enjoy this — daily geography puzzle just dropped." (Only quote-tweet if there's a genuine connection between their tweet and yours.)

Most won't engage back. 2–3 will. Those 2–3 become your first ambassadors.

**One last note**: in this niche, *one* RT from `@metrodoku` or `@globle_game` is worth 100 RTs from a generic account. Quality of audience > size.
