About SoFi Stadium

Newest US venue (opened 2020). $5.5B cost, most expensive stadium ever built.

SoFi Stadium is the newest US World Cup venue (opened September 2020). Cost: $5.5 billion, the most expensive stadium ever built. Has a translucent ETFE roof and a 70,000-seat capacity that can expand to 100,000 for special events.

Getting to Los Angeles

  • Airport: LAX, served by Metro Crenshaw/LAX line direct to SoFi.
  • Public transit: Metro K Line stops near SoFi.
  • Walking distance: Intuit Dome (LA Clippers' new home), Hollywood Park casino, YouTube Theater.
