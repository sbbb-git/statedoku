MetLife Stadium — your 2026 World Cup fan guide

Hosts the FINAL. NJ Transit from NYC. Hotels, food, parking, security.

MetLife Stadium in East Rutherford, New Jersey, hosts the 2026 FIFA World Cup Final on July 19, 2026, plus 7 other matches. Capacity: 82,500 — the largest US venue. Here's everything you need to know as a fan.

Address
1 MetLife Stadium Dr, East Rutherford, NJ
Capacity
82,500 (largest US WC venue)
Total WC matches
8 (including FINAL)
Opened
2010
Other tenants
NY Giants + NY Jets (NFL)

How to get there

From Manhattan (best option for most fans)

NJ Transit:
From Penn Station NYC, take the Meadowlands Rail Line directly to Meadowlands Sports Complex Station. 15 minutes, $5.25.
Trains run every 15 mins on event days.
Station is at the stadium gates.

From Newark Liberty Airport (EWR)

30-min NJ Transit ride from Newark Airport. Or 25-min Uber/Lyft ($35-60).

From JFK Airport

1h15 by train (AirTrain + LIRR + transfer) or 1h drive ($70-120 Uber/Lyft).

Driving

From NYC: I-95 N to Exit 17. Stadium parking $50+/day on match days.

Hotels nearby

Closest (East Rutherford / Carlstadt) — walking/short drive

  • Sheraton Lincoln Harbor — Weehawken, 10 mins drive
  • Hampton Inn East Rutherford — 1 mile from stadium
  • Embassy Suites Secaucus — 2 miles, NJ Transit access
  • Price range during WC: $350-$800/night

NYC (best vibe, cheaper than stadium hotels)

  • Times Square hotels — walk to Penn Station then 15-min train
  • Price range during WC: $250-$1,200/night depending on hotel

Food at the stadium

  • Standard NFL food: hot dogs, pretzels, beer
  • Premium options: Shake Shack, Carl's Cuban, gourmet pizza
  • Plant-based options available
  • Beer/wine standard. Hard liquor in club levels only

Best seats

For WC matches, FIFA categorizes seats 1-4:

  • Cat 1: 100-200 level, near field. $300+ group, $5K+ Final
  • Cat 2: Lower bowl corners. $200+ group, $3K+ Final
  • Cat 3: Upper bowl. $100+ group, $2K+ Final
  • Cat 4 (host residents only): Discount tier. Available only with US/Canada/Mexico address

Security & policies

  • Clear bag policy (12" x 12" or smaller, clear)
  • No outside food or drink
  • Arrive 60-90 mins before kickoff (security + queues)
  • Vibrant cashless: card only, no cash accepted

Frequently asked questions

How do I get to MetLife Stadium from Manhattan?

NJ Transit from Penn Station — Meadowlands Rail Line, direct to Meadowlands Sports Complex Station. 15 minutes, $5.25.

Where should I stay for the World Cup Final?

NYC offers the best vibe + value. Stay near Penn Station, ride NJ Transit 15 min. Stadium-area hotels (Hampton Inn, Embassy Suites) are pricier but walking distance.

Is parking available at MetLife?

Yes. ~$50+/day on WC days. Pre-purchase recommended via stadium website.

Can I bring my own food/drink?

No. Stadium is fully cashless and prohibits outside food/drink. Bottled water is provided free at certain stations.
