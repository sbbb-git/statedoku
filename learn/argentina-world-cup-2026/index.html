Argentina at the 2026 World Cup

Defending champions. Messi could play his 6th and possibly final World Cup at 39. Inter Miami connection means Hard Rock Stadium becomes practically a home venue.

The squad

Argentina's 2026 squad is built around: Lionel Messi, Lautaro Martínez, Julián Álvarez, Enzo Fernández, Alexis Mac Allister. Manager Lionel Scaloni has been refining the team for years.

Argentina's diaspora in the US

~285k Argentines in US. Concentrated in Miami (~70k post-Messi), NY/NJ (~40k), LA (~25k).

Messi's farewell?

Messi will be 39 during the tournament. Despite saying after 2022 that "I won't play another World Cup", he has not formally retired. If he plays, it's his record-tying 6th WC alongside Ronaldo. Inter Miami based him in the US — Hard Rock Stadium becomes Argentina's home venue.
