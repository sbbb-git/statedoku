Why Texas gets the most matches

Texas has 30M people, central US location, two massive NFL stadiums with indoor air conditioning. AT&T Stadium has the largest capacity (80,000) and a retractable roof. NRG Stadium is fully indoor. Both protect players and fans from the brutal Texas summer heat (June-July averages 95-105°F).

AT&T Stadium — Dallas (Arlington)

Home of the Dallas Cowboys. 80,000 capacity. Hosts 9 matches — the most of any US venue. Located in Arlington, between Dallas and Fort Worth. Retractable roof, indoor field. Hosts a Round of 16 and Quarter-Final.

Texas at the World Cup — the diaspora factor

  • Hispanic population: 12M (40% of Texas). Expect huge Mexican, Salvadoran, Honduran, and Argentine fan presence.
  • African population: Texas has the largest African immigrant population in the US (Nigerian, Ethiopian, Ghanaian communities concentrated in Houston).
  • European fans: Cheap flights from Frankfurt + London means Texas could see large traveling European supporter contingents.
