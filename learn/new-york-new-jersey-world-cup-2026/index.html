🏆 FIFA WORLD CUP 2026

New York / New Jersey, 2026 World Cup

8 matches at MetLife Stadium. Located in New Jersey. Hosts the FINAL.

New York / New Jersey, New Jersey is one of the 11 US host cities for the FIFA World Cup 2026, with all matches played at MetLife Stadium. The venue will host 8 matches, including the FINAL on July 19, 2026.

About MetLife Stadium

Hosts the FINAL on July 19, 2026. Located in East Rutherford, NJ, across the Hudson from Manhattan.

This venue hosts the World Cup Final on July 19, 2026. MetLife is the only US venue dedicated to a confirmed Final slot, making it the most coveted ticket of the tournament.

Getting to New York / New Jersey

  • Airports: Newark (EWR, closest to MetLife), JFK, LaGuardia (LGA).
  • Public transit: NJ Transit train from NYC Penn Station direct to Meadowlands station (15 min).
  • Walking distance from MetLife: Limited, most fans will base themselves in NYC (Manhattan).

What to know about New Jersey

Frequently asked questions

How many matches will New York / New Jersey host?

New York / New Jersey hosts 8 matches during the 2026 FIFA World Cup, all at MetLife Stadium.
