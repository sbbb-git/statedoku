🏆 ⚽ THE OFFICIAL BALL

The 2026 World Cup official ball

Connected ball technology, AI-assisted VAR, the manufacturer (Adidas), and the design.

FIFA and Adidas unveiled the official ball of the 2026 FIFA World Cup in November 2025. It builds on the connected-ball tech that debuted at Qatar 2022 (Al Rihla).

Key facts

  • Manufacturer: Adidas (their 14th consecutive WC ball)
  • Design: Tri-color motif honoring USA, Canada, Mexico
  • Technology: Connected Ball with 500Hz IMU sensor, contributing to AI-assisted VAR
  • Material: Polyurethane outer + textured surface for grip
  • Weight: 410-450g (FIFA standard)
  • Circumference: 68-70cm (FIFA standard)

Connected ball technology

The ball contains a 500Hz Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU) sensor at its center, sending data to VAR officials in real-time. This means:

  • Precise offside detection at the moment of contact
  • Faster handball decisions
  • Better understanding of foul moments in tight challenges
  • Real-time data for broadcast graphics

Design symbolism

The ball features three intersecting visual zones representing the three host countries: red zones for USA + Canada, green for Mexico, white connecting them. Subtle iconography includes:

  • Stars and stripes motif (USA)
  • Maple leaf pattern (Canada)
  • Aztec geometric design (Mexico)

Adidas's 14 consecutive WC balls

Adidas has supplied every official WC ball since 1970 — 14 consecutive tournaments. This is the longest manufacturer relationship in WC history.

How to buy the ball

Official 2026 WC ball replicas are sold at:

  • adidas.com — official retailer
  • FIFA Store at host venues + airports
  • Soccer.com, Dick's Sporting Goods, etc. — authorized retailers
  • Price: ~$165 retail, $40 for youth-size replica

Frequently asked questions

Who makes the 2026 World Cup ball?

Adidas — their 14th consecutive WC official ball, going back to 1970.

Does the 2026 ball have technology in it?

Yes. It contains a 500Hz Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU) sensor that sends data to FIFA's AI-assisted VAR system in real-time, enabling precise offside and foul detection.

How much does the official ball cost?

~$165 for an official replica adult ball, $40 for youth-size. Available at adidas.com and major US sports retailers.

When was the 2026 ball unveiled?

November 2025 — about 7 months before the tournament. Adidas typically announces a major launch event.
