Miami — 2026 World Cup

7 matches at Hard Rock Stadium. Located in Florida.

About Hard Rock Stadium

Getting to Miami

  • Airports: Miami International (MIA) or Fort Lauderdale (FLL).
  • Public transit: Limited — rideshare recommended to Hard Rock Stadium in Miami Gardens.
  • Walking distance: Not pedestrian-friendly — plan for 30-min drives between stadium, beach, and downtown.
