About AT&T Stadium

Hosts the most matches of any US venue. Located in Arlington, TX.

AT&T Stadium hosts the most matches of any US venue (9). It's an indoor retractable-roof venue — protecting players and fans from the Texas summer heat. Located in Arlington, between Dallas and Fort Worth.

Getting to Dallas

  • Airports: DFW International (DFW) or Dallas Love Field (DAL).
  • Public transit: Limited — drive or rideshare to AT&T Stadium in Arlington (between Dallas and Fort Worth).
  • Walking distance: Globe Life Field (Texas Rangers baseball stadium) next door.

How many matches will Dallas host?
