These maps are released free for personal, educational, and editorial use. Print as many copies as you need for your classroom or family. Attribution to statedoku.com appreciated but not required.

What's the best way to use these?

  • Quiz/worksheet: print the blank map, ask students to fill in state names. Score with the labeled version.
  • Memorization drill: print all 3 maps. Day 1 study the labeled version; days 2-7 quiz with the blank version.
  • Geography lesson: use the labeled map alongside the states + capitals table as a study handout.
  • Daily warm-up: 5 minutes of fill-in-the-blank to start the geography unit.
