World Cup 2026 — ticket prices by phase

Group stage $60-$300. R32 $80-$400. R16 $150-$500. Final $1K-$6K. The full breakdown.

FIFA released the 2026 World Cup ticket pricing in late 2025. Prices vary by phase, venue, and seat category. Here's the complete breakdown.

Resale market prices (typical)

Once first-phase tickets sold out, secondary market prices have moved:

  • Group stage: Up 30-100% above face value
  • R32 / R16: Up 50-150%
  • Quarter-Finals: Up 100-300%
  • Semi-Finals: Up 200-500%
  • Final: $5,000 - $30,000+ depending on teams

How to buy

Official channels

  • FIFA.com/tickets — primary sale, by random ballot during phases
  • FIFA Ticket Resale — official resale platform, post-purchase exchange
  • Hospitality packages — via FIFA partners (Wonder, MATCH Hospitality). $5K-$50K+ per match.

Secondary market (legal in USA)

  • StubHub — verified, anti-fraud protections
  • Vivid Seats — verified
  • SeatGeek — verified
  • Avoid: Craigslist, eBay, social media DMs — high fraud risk

Tips to save money

  • Buy via FIFA's last-minute ballot — leftover seats often go for face value
  • Target non-knockout matches — group-stage between mid-tier teams = cheapest
  • Avoid headliner teams — Brazil/Argentina/USA matches command premium prices
  • Avoid the Final — historically the highest-priced match. Watch in a fan zone or bar instead.

Categories

Within each phase, FIFA sells multiple seat categories:

  • Category 1: Closest to action. Premium prices.
  • Category 2: Lower bowl, slightly farther.
  • Category 3: Upper bowl.
  • Category 4 (host country residents only): Available only to people with US/Canada/Mexico billing addresses. Heavily discounted.

Frequently asked questions

How much is a 2026 World Cup ticket?

Group stage: $60-$300. Final: $1,000-$6,000. Resale prices can be much higher.

Where do I buy 2026 World Cup tickets?

Official: FIFA.com/tickets. Verified resale: StubHub, Vivid Seats, SeatGeek. Avoid unverified social media listings.

Are 2026 World Cup tickets sold out?

First-phase tickets sold out in October 2025. Subsequent phases (last-minute ballot, resale) remain available through the tournament.

How much do Final tickets cost?

Face value: $1,000-$6,000. Resale: $5,000-$30,000+ depending on which teams qualify.
