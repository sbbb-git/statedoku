About Statedoku

A tiny daily ritual that teaches you US geography.

Welcome to Statedoku — a free daily puzzle for anyone who likes US geography or wants to learn it. One grid a day, same one for everyone in the world. Solve it in five minutes, share your emoji grid, come back tomorrow.

The puzzle

Fill a 3×3 grid with US states. Each row and column has a constraint — "borders Mexico", "named after a king", "no state income tax". Every state must satisfy both its row and column. You get 3 mistakes. That's it.

Why we made it

Wordle proved that the right small ritual can teach you something every day without feeling like school. We wanted the same thing for American geography. After a month of Statedoku you'll know the 50 states cold — and a lot of weird trivia about them.

What makes it different

  • Every grid has exactly one solution. No guessing.
  • ~100 different constraints rotate — regions, borders, history, name origin, sports, mountains, belts. No two days feel the same.
  • Mobile-first. Tap a cell, type three letters, pick. Built for thumbs.
  • Three languages — English, French, Spanish.
  • No accounts required. Play instantly. Your progress saves locally on your device.

Get involved

Statedoku is a small project, built out of love for daily puzzles and US geography. If you spot a bug, find a constraint that feels unfair, have an idea for a new category, or want to help with translations — please write. We read every message and most good ideas end up shipped.
