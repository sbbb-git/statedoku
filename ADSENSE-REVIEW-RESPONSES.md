## How to actually reply

1. Log in to **adsense.google.com**.
2. The notification of the rejection / clarification request appears at the top of the dashboard.
3. Look for a **"Reply"** link or an email address (typically `adsense-noreply@google.com` — yes, you can email them back even though the address says "noreply").
4. Paste the relevant template above. Edit placeholders.
5. Be **factual and short**. Long defensive replies do not help. Stay polite.

## If they go silent for 6+ weeks

Open a support case via **AdSense Help** → **Contact us** → **AdSense application status**. Include your publisher ID `ca-pub-1481624152917622` and the date of submission.

---

## Tip: while waiting

Don't change the design, don't add new ad networks, don't remove pages. Keep publishing new content normally — your traffic graph is part of what Google looks at during review.

If you change the email address `contact@statedoku.com`, update this file and the privacy page accordingly.
