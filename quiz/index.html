How the quiz works

Each round picks 10 random questions across three categories: state capitals (which capital matches the state, or vice versa), regions (which region a state belongs to), and abbreviations (matching the 2-letter code to the state). Mixed mode rotates all three. Capitals-only, regions-only, and abbreviations-only let you drill a specific weakness.

Want to learn the states properly?

The 50 states guide walks you through every state by region in under a week. The capitals guide adds mnemonics for the 12 trickiest. The regions guide covers the Census Bureau's 4-region division.
